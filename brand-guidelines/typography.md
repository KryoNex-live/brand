# Typography

Typography contributes to the consistency and readability of the KryoNex brand.

## Principles

Typography should be:

- Clear
- Modern
- Professional
- Accessible
- Easy to read

## Recommendations

- Use consistent font families within a project.
- Maintain appropriate spacing.
- Preserve visual hierarchy using headings and body text.
- Avoid excessive font variations.

## Accessibility

Typography should remain readable across different screen sizes and devices.

## Future Expansion

Official font families and design specifications may be documented here as the design system evolves.
