# Colors

This document describes the KryoNex brand color system.

## Primary Brand Colors

The official color palette is defined by the KryoNex visual identity.

As the brand evolves, additional color values may be documented here.

## Usage Principles

- Maintain consistent color usage.
- Preserve accessibility and sufficient contrast.
- Avoid introducing unofficial colors into brand materials.
- Follow platform accessibility recommendations.

## Interface Design

Colors should support:

- Readability
- Accessibility
- Visual consistency
- Professional appearance

## Future Expansion

Additional color specifications (HEX, RGB, HSL, CMYK) may be added as the design system evolves.
